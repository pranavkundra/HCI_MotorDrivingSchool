Instructors

Our instructors have hand-crafted these lessons drawing on their vast years of learning experience as driving instructors and race car drivers.

Michael Schumacher

Veteran race car driver; 7 time Formula Won Champ! One of the greatest drivers of all time. Recently came out of a coma caused by a skiing accident and now teaches at ABS.

Sebastian Vettel

Known for his skill and speed both on and off the circuit, Sebastian Vettel proved his mettle by becoming the youngest Formula One Champion. Currently racing for Ferrari, he has taken time out to devise these lectures.

Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Hamilton was a rookie sensation who made waves right from his first year and scored the highest ever points scored by a rookie. Currently driving for the Mercedes team, he found time after his split with Nicole Scherzinger to help us out in designing these lectures.
